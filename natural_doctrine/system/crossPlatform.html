Cross Platform

NAtURAL DOCtRINE goes beyond the boundaries of normal PS4/PS3/PS Vita play and lets you enjoy total multiplayer freedom. Select "Multiplayer" from the Title Menu, then choose from either "Online" or "Ad Hoc" mode.




In the multiplayer version, you will collect different types of cards, then use them to build your own battle deck. Each card represents a unit with unique stats, equipment, and skills, just like in singleplayer mode. On top of that, each card has a size, cost, Pluton value, and rarity.



Multiplayer Flow
1) Build a deck out of your current cards.
2) Look for competitive or co-op rooms, or create your own.
3) Begin your mission.
4) Obtain Card Points (CP) and earn new cards.



Deck Creation Tips
- Each deck can only have a maximum Card Size of 8.
- Some cards have fractional sizes (e.g. 1.5).
- Each deck has a total cost limit.
- In co-op, only a mini-deck can be used.



When playing online, you can either search for rooms that have already been created, or you can make your own and wait for another player to join. You can also invite people from your friends list to play.




On top of simple victory or defeat, you will also earn CP based on mission goals, performance bonuses, and rank differential bonuses after each battle.

With your PS Vita, you can engage in ad hoc battles against your friends!

Ad hoc lets you put together battles on the fly, and any records or information will not be saved. You can take the decks you've created online to challenge friends in person and treat it like a friendly practice match!




You're free to enjoy the world of NAtURAL DOCtRINE without worrying about time or place! While you're at home, PS4! While you're on the go, PS Vita!

Save data is cross-compatible, so you can play on your PlayStation™4 or PlayStation®3 at home, then take your PS™Vita and play it wherever you go. Make sure you upload your data to PSN so you can access it anywhere!
