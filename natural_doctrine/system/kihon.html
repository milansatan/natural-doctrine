The Basics

You can switch between 3 different views. Each one will give you a different perspective on the battlefield and forces involved.







Depending on the battlefield, there may be cover objects, differences in elevation, and destructible elements.



Pluton is a rare material used to fuel magical spells, and is generally found deep within mines. When opening up treasure chests, along with weapons and accessories, you may also find Pluton. Your total amount of Pluton is shown in the top-right corner of the screen. The total amount of Pluton available throughout the game is fixed, so if you consume too much early on, you might not have enough later on, leading to a potential crisis. So be very aware of your Pluton use throughout the game.
