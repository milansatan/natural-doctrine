Characters





【Geoff】
Sex : Male
Race : Human
Role : Warrior





Geoff wields a shield and one-handed sword, and acts as a close-range attacker and tank. Early on, he learns valuable shield skills that allow him to greatly reduce the damage enemies can inflict. As a result, he's indispensable during the early stages of the game.。


  
  
















 




【Vasily】
Sex : Female
Race : Human
Role : Warrior





Just like Geoff, Vasily uses a sword-and-shield combo. Although she's not quite as tough as Geoff, she's still highly adept at dealing close-range damage. In situations where the enemy is flanking you or hits you with a surprise ambush, she can step in as a secondary tank to absorb the damage.



  
  















 




【Anka】
Sex : Female
Race : Human
Role : Command Medic





Anka has a special talent for dealing with things like locked doors or treasure chests that might trouble other characters. In battle, she serves an important role as the party's healer, and she can also provide ranged support on offense.


  
  









 






 




【Zekelinde】
Sex : Male
Species : Human
Role : Sword Master





Zeke is easily the most powerful offensive force on the team, sweeping away all but the toughest enemies. Eventually he develops into a useful tank as well, and becomes a core part of your formation.



  
  




 




 






 




【Mel】
Sex : Female
Species : Human
Role : Golem Handler





With a body literally made of steel, Mel's golem is the toughest tank around. It's most useful on the frontlines, absorbing enemy attacks and acting as the party's guardian angel, so to speak. Meanwhile, Mel can hang back and safely take aim at the enemy from a distance.


  
  




 














【Nebula】
Sex : Male
Species : Human
Role : Apprentice






Nebula harnesses the magical power of Pluton to fuel his arcane arts. With a variety of offensive and defensive spells, he becomes a powerful weapon when the group finds themselves in a tough situation. However, Pluton is a limited resource, so it's important to understand when to use it, and when to find another path to success.



  
  




 




 











【Tatiana】
Sex : Female
Species : Human
Role : Marksman





Tatyana is a firearms expert, so it's important to position her in a way that allows her to intercept enemies as soon as they come into view. She excels at landing critical hits, so it's possible to take out regular enemies with a single shot.
