Sub-races and Earth Tribes

This species overwhelms all the others when it comes to raw population. Most live within their mines, advancing a civilization very different from our own. They excel at extracting minerals and other resources from the earth, as well as manipulating metals and other materials. As a result, they're constantly clashing with humans, who covet their technological skill.




Due to their history of constant conflict with humanity, goblins will attack any human they see without hesitation. It's assumed that there are different types or ranks of goblins, based on the different gear they wear.


This violent species embraces a feudal society in which power is everything. They've constructed a large outpost in the wilderness to use as their base of operations. Because they value physical strength most of all, they see all other species as inferior, especially humans.



Even the lowliest orc has the same battle prowess as a veteran human warrior. In order to stand against them, you must accept the very real risk of death.


The lizardfolk follow a strict set of religious precepts and maintains a pyramid-shaped class-based society. Contact with humans is rare, and their heightened intellect has allowed them to build an elaborate civilization within ancient ruins.

Lizardfolk soldiers are a well-trained battle group skilled at using both swords and magic. They work as a single powerful unit to defend their kingdom.


This massive beast has the head of a bull and the body of a man. Its body is so tough it can't be pierced with any common blade. It has almost no intelligence, but still displays a brutal, ferocious personality.

Its imposing frame is enough to overwhelm even your golem ally. One swing of its hammer can down any creature.


These reptilian beasts come swooping in one after another under the cover of a snowstorm as they rush to defend their sacred home. In ancient times, they have joined with other species to protect the land, and can destroy any enemy with their thunder breath and cyclone attacks.


Deep within the scorching hot northern caves, these scaly beasts band together to beat back intruders. Their fire breath acts as a powerful ranged attack that fills the air with hellfire.


The Ancient Dragon comes from a time before time, and has removed himself from the world's ecosystem. His power is equal to that of a natural disaster, but its habitat is unknown to most and human contact is exceedingly rare.

When the Ancient Dragon decides to take to the air, your standard swords and shields are no match.
